## 👋 About Me

I'm an **M.Sc. student in Intelligent Adaptive Systems at Universität Hamburg** with six years of industry experience at the intersection of **healthcare AI** and **full-stack engineering**. My work pairs peer-reviewed research (IEEE TPDS, 200+ citations) with shipped products that real clinicians use every day.

I move comfortably between two worlds: I can prototype a transformer for clinical text in PyTorch in the morning and ship a typed React + Laravel feature behind it in the afternoon. That dual fluency is what I bring to every team — research that survives contact with production, and products that respect the methods underneath them.

> 🎯 **Currently:** Building an AI-driven premedication pipeline with UKE Hamburg as my master's project, and writing PhD applications targeting research groups in **Applied NLP, Clinical AI, and Trustworthy ML** across Germany.

---

## 🔬 Research Interests

I'm drawn to problems where **language understanding meets high-stakes decisions** — clinical text, requirements engineering, software analytics. My ideal PhD sits in one of these spaces:

| Area | What excites me |
|---|---|
| 🧠 **Applied NLP & LLMs** | Domain-adapted transformers, retrieval-augmented generation, evaluation beyond benchmarks |
| 🏥 **Clinical & Healthcare AI** | Decision-support systems, clinical text mining, GDPR-compliant ML pipelines |
| 🛡️ **Trustworthy & Human-Centered AI** | Calibration, explainability, robustness under distribution shift |
| ⚙️ **AI for Software Engineering** | NLP on requirements & code, developer-facing intelligent assistants |

**Active master's research:** *UKE AI Pipeline for Smarter Premedication* — designing and optimising data pipelines that turn unstructured pre-operative records into structured signals for downstream ML. Supervised within the Universitätsklinikum Hamburg-Eppendorf (UKE) ecosystem.
